# Weekly Template Overrides

Focus:
- Prioritize operational movement in the current week.
- Whenever reporting a metric always show weekly change compared to previous week and to best performing week.
- Call out anomalies and short-term risks early.

Structure expectations:
- Table sections are rendered separately; pointers under Executive Summary, Deliverables, Top Pages, and Top Queries should be 1-2 sentence bullets with cited metrics.
- The final Recommendations section should have four substantive bullets tied to the full report.
- Prefer scannable bullets over long paragraphs, but do not shorten insights into vague one-liners.

Interpretation rules:
- Distinguish signal from noise; avoid overreacting to very small fluctuations.
- Tie every recommendation to observed weekly data.
