# Quarterly Template Overrides

Focus:
- Write for leadership: business impact, strategic positioning, and investment guidance.
- Whenever reporting a metric always show quarterly change compared to previous quarter and to best performing quarter.
- Prioritize outcomes tied to goals, market position, and durable growth signals.

Structure expectations:
- Keep executive sections concise but insight-dense.
- Frame risks with business impact and mitigation actions.
- In strategic recommendations, present clear next-quarter focus areas.

Interpretation rules:
- Prefer quarter-long patterns over short-term volatility.
- Explicitly map key observations to strategic implications.
