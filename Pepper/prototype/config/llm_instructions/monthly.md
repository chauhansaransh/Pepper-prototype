# Monthly Template Overrides

Focus:
- Emphasize trend direction and strategic learnings across the month.
- Whenever reporting a metric always show monthly change compared to previous month and to best performing month.
- Connect channel performance with outcomes (engagement and conversions).

Structure expectations:
- Use narrative where the section type expects synthesis, but keep claims data-backed.
- In recommendations, separate quick wins from strategic bets.

Interpretation rules:
- Highlight sustained wins and persistent underperformance, not one-off spikes.
- Explain likely drivers only when supported by provided context.
